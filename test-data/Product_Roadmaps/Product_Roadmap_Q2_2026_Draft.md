## Likely Priority 1: AI Lead Scoring Engine
**Earliest start:** April 2026 (pending CRM data quality work in Q1)  

Scoring engine moved to Q2 after deferral from Q1. Precondition: data engineering must complete CRM data backfill for 2021–2022 records by March 31. If this slips, scoring engine moves to Q3.

---

## Likely Priority 2: Mobile App General Availability
**Condition:** Beta NPS must be > 40 to proceed  

Mobile app has been in beta since Q3 2025. If Q1 beta NPS exceeds 40, GA launch in Q2 with a proper go-to-market campaign. If NPS is below 40, a second iteration round is needed before GA.

---

## Likely Priority 3: Self-Serve Onboarding Redesign
**Context:** Time to first value currently 18 days vs 10-day target  

One of our most persistent product problems. New customer onboarding is too complex — requires CS team involvement for 3 of the first 5 steps. A self-serve redesign targeting < 10 days time to value is planned for Q2.
