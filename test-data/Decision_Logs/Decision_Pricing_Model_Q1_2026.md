## Decision

Revise pricing tiers for new customers starting February 1, 2026. Existing customers grandfathered at current pricing for 12 months, then migrated with 90-day notice.

---

## New Pricing Structure

| Plan | Old Price | New Price | Key Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | $49/month | $59/month | +$10 (+20%) |
| Growth | $149/month | $199/month | +$50 (+34%) |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Minimum $2,000/month (was $1,200) |

---

## What the Analysis Showed

Meera conducted a pricing review in November–December 2025. Key findings:

**We are underpriced relative to competitors:**
- Intercom Growth equivalent: $249/month
- HubSpot equivalent tier: $214/month
- Our Growth plan: $149/month — 30–40% below market

**Our value delivery has increased significantly:**
- Since last pricing update (2023), we shipped Unified Inbox, Analytics Redesign, Mobile App
- Q1 2026 adds Workflow Automation and Custom Dashboards
- Customer ROI interviews show average $8,400/month in saved time and recovered revenue — our pricing captures < 2% of delivered value

**Price sensitivity analysis:**
- Ran 3 pricing experiments with new prospects in October–November using different price points
- Conversion rate at $199/month Growth was 4% lower than at $149/month
- Expansion revenue modelling shows the 4% conversion loss is offset by 34% higher ARPU within 6 months

---

## What Was Not Changed

- **Free trial:** Remains 14 days, no credit card required. Research showed removing the free trial reduced top-of-funnel by 22% — not worth the revenue risk.
- **Annual discount:** Remains 20% for annual commitment.
- **Starter plan feature set:** No features removed from Starter — price increase only.

---

## Grandfathering Policy

Existing customers will be notified December 20 via in-app message and email. Key terms:
- Current pricing locked for 12 months (until February 2027)
- 90-day notice before migration to new pricing
- Customers on annual plans: price change applies at next renewal after February 2027

Rohan flagged that CS team needs talking points before the notification goes out. Meera to deliver by December 19.

---

## Risk Assessment

- **Churn risk:** Estimated 3–5% of existing customers may churn when migrated to new pricing in 2027. At current ARR, this is $108K–$180K at risk. Acceptable given $600K+ upside from new customer pricing.
- **Sales objection risk:** Tanya briefed sales team December 19. Positioning: "We've added significant value since 2023 — this is the first price update in 3 years, and we're still below market."
- **Competitor response risk:** Low — competitors are already at these price points.
