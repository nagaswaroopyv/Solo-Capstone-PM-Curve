# Presentation: Q1 2026 Product Roadmap
## Board Update — January 2026
**Presenter:** Meera Iyer, CPO  
**Audience:** Board of Directors  
**Date:** January 8, 2026  

---

## Slide 1: Where We Are

**Q3 2025: A wake-up call.**

We missed ARR target by 14%. Lost 3 enterprise accounts. Post-mortem was clear: we have a strong core product but we're losing deals on features our competitors have had for years.

The team did not celebrate. We diagnosed. We planned. We are executing.

---

## Slide 2: What Our Customers Need From Us

Exit interview analysis from 8 churned accounts in H2 2025:

- **"Your automation is too manual"** — 6 of 8 accounts
- **"We need self-serve reporting"** — 5 of 8 accounts  
- **"Your support response time is too slow"** — 4 of 8 accounts

These are not product vision problems. These are product execution gaps. Q1 closes them.

---

## Slide 3: Q1 2026 Priorities

### 1. Workflow Automation Engine → Ships March 28
If-this-then-that automation built natively on our data model. 8 trigger types, 12 action types. No external tools required. Decision to build natively vs. Zapier partnership made in November — build wins on economics and strategic moat.

**Why it matters:** Came up in 8 of 12 lost deals in Q3. Sales has 4 late-stage deals where this is the stated blocker. Potential ARR recovery: $240K.

### 2. Custom Dashboard Builder → Ships February 14
No-code drag-and-drop dashboards for non-technical stakeholders. Pre-approved metric catalogue ensures data consistency. Self-serve replaces the 14 weekly report requests currently backlogged with the analytics team.

**Why it matters:** Demo win rate increases when prospects can see self-serve analytics. Marketing and CS leads have been asking for this for 18 months.

### 3. Unified Inbox v3: WhatsApp + SMS → Ships April 11
Extends existing inbox to cover WhatsApp Business and SMS. AI-suggested replies. Smart routing. Directly addresses the response time problem cited in churn exits.

**Why it matters:** Our fastest-growing customer segment (CS-heavy accounts) manages customer conversations across 3 channels today. We can consolidate that.

---

## Slide 4: What We're Not Building in Q1

**AI Lead Scoring Engine — moved to Q2.**
High build complexity requires clean historical CRM data. Pre-2023 CRM data has gaps. Data engineering is fixing this in Q1. Scoring engine starts in Q2 on clean data.

**Mobile App GA — conditional Q2 launch.**
Beta continues. If beta NPS > 40 at end of Q1, we launch in Q2 with full go-to-market. If below 40, another iteration round.

**Salesforce native integration — Q3 at earliest.**
Frequently requested by enterprise. On the roadmap but not in Q1 — team is at capacity.

---

## Slide 5: Pricing Update

Effective February 1, 2026, new pricing for new customers:
- Starter: $49 → $59/month
- Growth: $149 → $199/month
- Enterprise: minimum $2,000/month (was $1,200)

We were 30–40% below market. First price increase since 2023. Existing customers grandfathered for 12 months.

**Revenue impact:** Modelled +$380K ARR from new customers in first 12 months at new pricing.
